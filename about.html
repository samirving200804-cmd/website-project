About Me

Hello — I'm Sam Irving, a digital design student who creates branding and packaging. I found this interest later in life after I had finished school and still didn't know what I wanted to do with my professional life. I decided to download illustrator out of boredom, and immediately fell in love with the process of creating and designing. Since then, I've been honing my skills and working on various projects that allow me to express my creativity and passion for design. feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate on a project!
